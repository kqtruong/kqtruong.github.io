Our Services

Web Development

Sign-up Form

We are currently offering free web hosting and sub-domains to participating High School clubs and non-profit groups. We hope that this service will initially serve as a sandbox for learning and web development, but will flourish into something more. We want to spark their interest in utilizing and learning other technologies, like coding for mobile apps, the cloud, and IoT.

Social Hub

Affliated Volunteering Groups

We want to provide a platform where clubs can interact and network with one another. They can learn from one another by viewing each others web pages and sharing volunteering opportunities.
